A Beginner's Guide to 5e D&D

Classes Races Combat Resources

Fifth Edition Dungeons and Dragons is the 2014 redeign of the iconic roleplaying game. This guide will explain some of the most confusing concepts in D&D including the classes, the races, the character sheet and combat.

All official games and images come courtesy of Wizards of the Coast's Fan Content Policy and was compiled by Adam Turner. Code is available here on GitHub.
